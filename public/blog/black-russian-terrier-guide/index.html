A Guide to the Black Russian Terrier

July 1, 2025

The Black Russian Terrier (BRT), or “Tchiorny Terrier,” is a powerful and intelligent breed developed in the Soviet Union. Known as the “Black Pearl of Russia,” this is not a true terrier but rather a member of the working group, bred for guarding and military purposes.

Temperament and Personality

A properly bred and socialized BRT is calm, confident, and courageous. They are deeply loyal to their family and can be aloof with strangers, making them excellent watchdogs. Their intelligence is remarkable, but it comes with a streak of independence. Consistent, positive reinforcement-based training is essential from a young age.

Care and Grooming

The BRT has a dense, double coat that requires significant grooming to prevent matting. Regular brushing (several times a week) and professional grooming every 6-8 weeks are necessary. They are a large, active breed that needs regular exercise to stay physically and mentally fit.
